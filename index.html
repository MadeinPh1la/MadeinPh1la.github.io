National Impact

I developed the "Operation Predator" app for U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement on iOS and Android, available in the Apple App Store and Google Play from 2013 to 2019. The app was the first in federal law enforcement to combat child exploitation by crowdsourcing the ability to identify and locate child predator fugitives with warrants for their arrest. The app contributed to 12 arrests.
